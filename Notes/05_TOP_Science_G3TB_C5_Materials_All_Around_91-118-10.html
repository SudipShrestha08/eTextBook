Some objects are made from one material.

A ruler made of wood

Test tubes made of glass

A blanket made of cotton

Bands made of rubber

Some objects are made from more than one material.

Seat made of leather

Tyre made of rubber

Frame made of metal

Handle made of plastic

Main body made of cloth

Zip made of metal

A bicycle may be made of leather, rubber and metal. A school bag may be made of metal, plastic and cloth.

What materials do you think we can recycle?

Can we get back the materials used to make an object? Yes, we can! Some (but not all) objects can be broken down into the materials they were made of. The materials are used to make new objects. This is known as recycling. Start recycling today!
